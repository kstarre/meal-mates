We Can Live Better.

Taking the time and effort to learn to cook regularly can have the single most positive impact on your health and quality of life. People who cook at home more often, rather than eating out, have healthier overall diets with lower food expenses. At Meal Mates we understand the difficulties that come along with our busy and hectic schedules which often prevent people from preparing their own nutritious meals. We aim to fix this.

Sharing meals is not only good, it's good for you.

Meal Mates is a small-scale, food-sharing, social-networking, mobile focused app designed to bring groups of people together for dining convenience. We bring the accessibility of meal planning, made easy, to busy people. When you sign up you can join clubs and alternate food preparation for different days of the work week with friends and coworkers to strengthen relationships, reduce food waste, save time and money, be healthier, and live your best life.

Eco Friendly

Cooking at home is good for your health and for the environment. Meal Mates eliminates the issue of food waste from leftovers which many individuals cite to be one of the primary reasons they avoid cooking at home. Choosing to cook rather than eat out helps by reducing packaging waste and energy costs.

Make Friends

The sharing of food has always been part of the human story, all over the world, people say they make friends by "breaking bread together." If social bonds and friendships are key to a positive work environment, then sharing meals simply provide the opportunity for those bonds to form or strengthen.

Save Time

Meal Mates brings convenience to meal planning for busy people. We understand the innate desire to want to eat healthier by cooking at home on a regular basis doesn't always line up with a hectic schedule. This quandary can be eliminated through the use of our application.
